1
Growth Corridor One

Sultanpur Road — The Strategic Spine

Sultanpur Road is Lucknow's most strategically positioned growth corridor. It connects the city to the Purvanchal Expressway and serves as the spine along which the city's eastward expansion is actively occurring.

🛣️

Outer Ring Road Connectivity

Sultanpur Road intersects directly with Lucknow's Outer Ring Road project — a 104-km expressway that will dramatically reduce commute times and open the corridor to city-level commercial development.

🏗️

Purvanchal Expressway Access

Direct access to the Purvanchal Expressway makes Sultanpur Road properties highly desirable for families and businesses looking to benefit from pan-UP connectivity at reduced land prices.

🎓

Education Hub Proximity

Multiple engineering colleges, polytechnic institutes, and a growing cluster of CBSE schools along the Sultanpur Road corridor generate sustained demand for residential land in the area.

🏥

Healthcare Infrastructure

New multi-speciality hospitals and medical institutions are operational and under development — a key driver of residential demand and long-term land value appreciation.

🏭

Defence Corridor Proximity

The Uttar Pradesh Defence Industrial Corridor's Lucknow node is creating tens of thousands of skilled jobs in the region, directly fuelling residential demand in adjacent corridors including Sultanpur Road.

🏘️

Township & Planned Development

Multiple large-format township projects by private developers are underway along the Sultanpur Road axis — collectively validating the long-term investment thesis for the corridor.

2
Growth Corridor Two

Raebareli Road — The Emerging Powerhouse

Raebareli Road represents an early-stage investment opportunity with all the indicators of a growth trajectory similar to what Sultanpur Road experienced five years ago. The window at current price points is actively closing.

🚂

Rail & Highway Connectivity

Raebareli Road connects Lucknow to Raebareli, a key rail junction. The corridor benefits from both NH-30 highway access and multiple rail lines, making land here logistically desirable.

🏭

Industrial & MSME Clusters

Established MSME clusters and growing industrial units along the Raebareli Road create a stable employment base — translating directly into sustained residential plot demand in the area.

🖥️

IT & ITES Growth

Lucknow's expanding IT sector is generating a well-compensated workforce actively seeking residential investments in accessible, legally sound locations outside the city core.

🏫

Educational Institutions

A growing cluster of institutions including RMLAU and affiliated colleges generates consistent residential demand — creating a dual-use opportunity for both end-use and rental investment.

📍

Below-Market Entry Prices

Raebareli Road currently offers one of Lucknow's most attractive price-to-appreciation ratios. Early buyers in 2020–22 have already seen significant returns — the opportunity still exists in select pockets.

🏗️

Government Development Projects

Multiple government-backed infrastructure projects are in active stages of development along the Raebareli Road corridor — a reliable indicator of future appreciation and improved connectivity.

Common Questions

Before You Call Us,
Here's What Buyers Ask

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
Every enquiry receives a personal response within 2 hours during business hours. For WhatsApp messages, typically within 30 minutes.
Can I see legal documents before deciding?
Absolutely — and we insist on it. All Jila Panchayat and Nagar Panchayat approval documents are shared at the first meeting, before any payment discussion begins.
Do you charge for site visits?
Never. Site visits are completely free. Walk the land, ask questions, and take your time — there is no pressure at any stage.
What if I am buying from outside Lucknow or abroad?
We regularly assist NRI and outstation buyers. We arrange site visit videos, digital document verification, and coordinate with trusted local contacts on your behalf.
Are there payment plan options?
Yes. Flexible payment structures are available depending on the project. Full details are in our project brochure — request one via WhatsApp for instant delivery.
What does "Jila Panchayat approved" mean?
It means the land has received formal land-use approval from the district authority, making it legally recognised for residential construction. We verify this independently before listing any property.

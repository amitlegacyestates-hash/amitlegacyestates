Founder & Group CEO

Amit Tiwari

Amit Tiwari founded Amit Legacy Estates with a single, unwavering conviction: that families in Lucknow deserve to buy property with full confidence — not with doubt, confusion, or the fear of being misled.

With over 8 years working at the ground level of Lucknow's real estate market, Amit observed a pattern that troubled him deeply: genuine, hardworking families were making the most significant financial decision of their lives — guided by agents who prioritised commissions over client outcomes.

His response was not just a business — it was a philosophy. Every property under the Amit Legacy Estates banner is legally verified, Jila Panchayat or Nagar Panchayat approved, and shared with full documentation at the very first client meeting. No verbal promises. No hidden costs. No pressure.

Today, Amit Tiwari leads a firm that has become synonymous with transparency on Sultanpur Road and Raebareli Road — corridors he knows with the depth of someone who has walked every development zone, understood every approval process, and sat across the table with hundreds of families making life-defining decisions.

"My goal has never been to close a sale. It has always been to earn the kind of trust that brings a client back five years later and says: that was the right decision." — Amit Tiwari, Founder & Group CEO
